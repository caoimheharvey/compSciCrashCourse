Founded in 2014, Corner House is the articulation of a culinary vision by creative restaurateur and wine aficionado Renny Heng.
Its name references the eminent botanist E.J.H. Corner, whose former abode is now a fabled, irreplaceable Black and White
Colonial House and the sophisticated setting for a Michelin-starred restaurant. Its location in the UNESCO World Heritage
Site Botanic Gardens sets the stage for a multi-layered meal that combines its storied past, the botanical bounty of the world,
and a future that looks towards exhilarating, modern French-Asian interpretations by Executive Chef David Thien from Bordeaux.This is a new line
